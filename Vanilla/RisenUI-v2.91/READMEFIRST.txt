IMPORTANT: BACKUP INTERFACE FOLDER AND WTF FOLDER TO A SAFE PLACE BEFORE FOLLOWING THESE STEPS 
1) Remove your Interface folder and use the one provided in the zip
2) Remove your WTF folder and use the one provided in the zip
3) Clear game cache (delete the WDB folder)
4) Open up config.wtf located in the WTF folder with notepad and set your desired resolution (1920x1080 default) - DO NOT SKIP THIS STEP
5) Rename the Kronos folder inside the WTF folder to the realm you are currently playing on.  (Kronos II , Elysium, The Rebirth, Warsong, Naxxramas, Kul'Tiras etc)
6) Rename ACCOUNTNAME folder inside the WTF folder to your account name in all caps
7) Rename CHARACTERNAME folder inside the WTF folder to your characters name exactly how you see it in game
Note: Make copies of your main's folder and rename to alts if required*****
8) Run the game & adjust video, sound & interface settings
9) Rebind your actionbars and create your macros 
10) Log out properly to save your new settings

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you are not using 1920x1080 your addons will be configured to the right scale but MIGHT need to be moved to the right spot
on the UI. below I have provided the resources to make this as quick and painless as possible.

To make your life easier while you configure Luna, Bartender2, Big Wigs, Prat I have loaded FuBar plugins, just right click 
the bottom panel and activate them as I have them hidden by default.  You can also use the commands below.
